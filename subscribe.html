Connecting this form to a real inbox

Right now this form just shows a success message — it doesn't save emails anywhere. To actually collect signups, pick one option:

Easiest

Formspree or Getform

Free tools that email you every signup. Sign up, get a form endpoint URL, and set it as the form's action attribute.

Built for lists

ConvertKit or beehiiv

Made for creators sending updates. They give you an embeddable signup form or an endpoint to swap into this page.

All-in-one

Mailchimp

Free tier included. Create an audience, grab the embedded form code, and drop it into this page in place of the demo form.
